Holland on top

The Netherlands, aka Holland, is know for his football. Holland had some amazing legends in the world of football, like Johan Cruyff, Dennis Berkamp and many more. Eventhough they lost the world cup final in 2010 to Spain, they still became the world number 1. After a couple years the Dutch reached a remarkeble record.

The downfall

Holland were doing so great in the begin of the 21st centurie, but after 2011 it went downhill. People had no worries about the Netherlands qualifing for the European cup in 2017, but they lost to many games. Not qualifing for the EK would also result in not qualifing to the world cup. After a while there was light at the end of the tunnel.

Upcomming

Holland lost all his good players due aging, but a new generetion of talented footballers shown their mark. The Netherlands showed a remarkeble prestation in UEFA Nations League. Defeating the top countries in Europe. Most recently are the qualifiers for the European Cup in 2021, but the Dutch are starting more than great.
